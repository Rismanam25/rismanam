About Us

At RisManam, we believe everyone deserves a space to be heard — no judgment, no pressure.

We're a supportive community built to help you talk through stress, emotions, or just your day — with someone who truly listens. We're not therapists, and we're not here to give advice. We're here to create a safe, private, and comfortable space where you can express yourself freely.

Whether you're feeling overwhelmed, anxious, or just need someone to talk to — we’re here for you.

We're a small startup with a big heart, working to make emotional support more human, more accessible, and more affordable — one meaningful conversation at a time.

Our Mission

To make emotional support simple, affordable, and stigma-free for everyone — no matter where they are or what they're going through.

We’re building a safe space where people can have honest, judgment-free conversations with trained listeners who truly care. Whether it's stress, loneliness, or just needing someone to talk to — we're here for that.

Our mission is to empower people to feel heard, supported, and emotionally stronger — one mindful talk at a time.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a world where emotional support is as normal and accessible as talking to a friend — anytime, anywhere.

We imagine a future where seeking a calm, understanding conversation isn’t seen as weakness — but as strength. A future where more people feel connected, seen, and emotionally balanced.

Through technology and community, we aim to build a safe haven where anyone can reach out, breathe a little easier, and feel a little lighter — one mindful convo at a time.
